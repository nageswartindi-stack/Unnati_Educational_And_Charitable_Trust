About Unnati Educational and Charitable Trust

Our Mission: Unnati Trust empowers lives through education, compassion, and care. We support the underprivileged with food, clothing, and life-skill training while promoting women's hygiene and mental well-being to build a positive and thriving society.

Our Vision: "To create communities where good health, hygiene, and education are accessible to all, ensuring a brighter and safer tomorrow."

Founded in 2021 during the COVID pandemic, UNNATI began as a simple giveaway event by generous individuals. What started as a one-time act of kindness grew into a registered Trust in 2024, expanding our contributions to include meal distribution, blanket drives, educational kits, and hygiene kits for underprivileged communities.

Our Journey

UNNATI began in 2021 in Kalaburagi during the COVID pandemic, with a simple giveaway event by a group of generous and like-minded individuals. What started as a one-time act of kindness soon grew into a continuous commitment to uplift society.

We began by offering Future-Ready Skills training programs for underprivileged students, covering confidence building, study skills, and career awareness. So far, 50+ programs have benefitted over 2,500 students.

Mental Health Awareness has been one of our key focus areas throughout this journey.

In 2024, UNNATI evolved into a registered Trust, expanding its contributions to include:

  • 🍽️ Meal distribution for underprivileged communities
  • 🧥 Blanket drives to protect families from harsh weather
  • 📚 Educational kits with essential school supplies
  • 🧴 Hygiene kits promoting women's health and dignity

Today, we continue to create opportunities that uplift the quality of our communities by improving their lives through education, compassion, and care.

Our Programs

Educational Programs

Our Educational Kit Distribution provides essential school supplies to underprivileged children. Through Career Readiness and Success Builder Programs, we empower 2,500+ students with life skills, memory techniques, exam stress management, and goal-oriented mindsets.

Health & Hygiene Programs

Our Women Hygiene Awareness Camps educate 100+ women on menstrual hygiene and provide essential kits. The Mental Health Awareness Program encourages emotional wellness, while our Community Food Drives have fed 2,000+ people through 20 meal distributions.

Community Support Programs

Our Seasonal Clothes Donation & Blanket Distribution Program has kept 200+ families warm. We collaborate with NGOs like Azim Premji Foundation and SDF Foundation to create lasting impact through charitable activities and teacher trainings.
