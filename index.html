About Unnati Educational and Charitable Trust

We support local leaders and organisations to deliver effective programs in education, health and livelihoods — focused on measurable outcomes and sustainability.

At Unnati Educational and Charitable Trust, we are committed to uplifting communities through compassionate action. We distribute warm clothes to elderly people during winters, donate books and educational supplies to school children to foster learning, and organize free health camps to provide medical care and awareness. Our efforts extend to food distribution, skill development workshops, and emergency aid, ensuring no one is left behind.

Our Programs

Education Support

We donate books, educational items, and stationery to school kids in underserved communities. Our programs focus on access, retention, and quality education through scholarships and early learning initiatives.

Health & Wellness

Conducting free health camps for preventive care, vaccinations, and medical check-ups. We also distribute essential items like clothes to elderly people, ensuring dignity and comfort in their later years.

Livelihoods & Community Support

Skills training, microfinance, and small business support to empower families. We provide clothing distributions, food aid, and other essentials to vulnerable groups, fostering sustainable economic growth.
